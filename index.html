Featured Projects

GenoPath

GenoPath, a streamlined pipeline that integrates existing computational methods for tumor evolution analysis, along with novel visualization tools to support biological interpretation. GenoPath is designed to reduce computational barriers and enable broader access to complex analyses in cancer genomics. The pipeline is openly available via GitHub, and the manuscript includes a real-world case study, code description, and discussion of the tool's utility and limitations.

Publication: GenoPath: A pipeline to infer tumor clone composition, mutational history, and metastatic cell migration events from tumor DNA sequencing data.
Journal: Frontiers in Bioinformatics, 2025
Authors: Ryan Tobin, Shikha Singh, Sudhir Kumar, Sayaka Miura
